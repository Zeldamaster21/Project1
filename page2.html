A bit of Info on Rugby

  • You pass the ball backwards always
  • you have to tackle from the waist down
  • there are 30 total players in the field
  • there is some part of padding your allowed to use
  • you cannot use cleats that have cleats in the front of the shoe
  • You could kick the ball forward as long as the rest of your team is behind you
  • you cannot slap the ball in midair, or push the other person while trying to get the ball
  • fun fact! the players will never argue with the ref
